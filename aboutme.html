    ABOUT ME
     

    Hey there! I'm Alagappan Sellappan, and I am a rising high school senior based out of the Greater Richmond Area in Central Virginia. In addition to being a student, I am also the director of technology focusing on the technical aspects of a 501(c)(3) non-profit organization, database manager at a startup company built for students by students, president of my school's coding club, webmaster at other school-based organizations, and a whole assortment of other projects.

    My passion is to help people with the skillsets under my toolbelt. Tying this with my interest of analyzing data and the biological sciences, I aspire to become a computational biologist/ bioinformatician with a path to get a Ph.D in Computer Science and then heading into the technology workforce.

    When I'm not clacking my keyboard, I am usually hanging out with family and friends (virtually of course) or watching my favorite crime-based shows on Netflix or CBS.
